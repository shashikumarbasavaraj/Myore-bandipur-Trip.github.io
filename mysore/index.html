MYSORE-BANDIPUR TRIP

Explore the Wild – Bandipur Safari Adventure and Mysore Heritage Awaits!

Yatra Mithra

Journey Details from Mysore

10:00 PM | Friday Night - Day 0

  • 🚍 Pickup from Mysore Railway Station/Bus Stand.
  • 🌌 Check-in to 3-star hotel at 10 PM (Friday night).
  • 🛏️ Night stay to relax for the adventure ahead.

6:00 AM | Saturday Morning - Day 1

  • 🌄 6:30 AM: Begin a refreshing trek to Chamundi Hills, climbing the iconic 1,000 steps.
  • 🛕 8:30 AM: Visit the sacred Chamundeshwari Temple, embracing Mysore’s spiritual essence.
  • 🍴 9:30 AM: Enjoy a delightful breakfast at Mylary Hotel, relishing their famous Idly and Dosa.
  • 🏨 10:00 AM: Return to the hotel, unwind for 30 minutes, and prepare to check out.
  • 🏰 11:00 AM: Marvel at the opulence of Mysore Palace, a testament to royal grandeur.
  • ⛪ 12:00 PM: Explore St. Philomena's Church, showcasing its stunning architectural charm.
  • 🚗 1:00 PM: Visit the fascinating Payana Car Museum, a haven for automobile enthusiasts.
  • 🍴 2:30 PM: Relish the authentic flavors of Mysore with lunch at Hanumanthu Palav.
  • ☕ 4:00 PM: Take a break to sip on refreshing tea and rejuvenate for the evening.
  • 🦜 5:00 PM: Discover unique exhibits at the Birds Museum, a treat for nature lovers.
  • 🛍️ 6:00-7:00 PM: Enjoy leisure shopping for souvenirs and delightful local treasures.
  • 🌟 7:00 PM: Experience the enchanting Mysore Palace Light Show, a visual spectacle.
  • 🚐 8:00 PM: Embark on your scenic journey to Bandipur, embracing the night’s charm.
  • 🏡 9:00 PM: Arrive at the resort for dinner, poolside fun, dancing, and a cozy fire camp.

Journey Details from Bandipur

6:00 AM | Sunday Morning - Day 2

  • 🌅 5:30 AM: Freshen up, enjoy morning tea, and head to the safari starting point.
  • 🐾 6:00 - 7:00 AM: Experience a thrilling jungle safari, spotting wildlife and enjoying nature.
  • 🍴 8:00 AM: Have breakfast and check out of the resort.
  • 🛕 9:00 AM: Visit Gopalaswamy Temple, perched at 4,770 feet above sea level.
  • 🛕 11:00 AM: Explore the sacred Dakshina Kashi Nanjundeshwara Temple.
  • 🍴 1:30 PM: Enjoy a delicious lunch.
  • 🛕 3:00 PM: Visit Venugopalaswamy Temple in Mysore.
  • 🌇 5:00 PM: Enjoy the serene sunset at Mysore's backwaters.
  • 🌟 6:30 PM: Witness the magical water and light dance at Brindavan Gardens.
  • 🚍 8:00 PM: Trip concludes with a drop-off at the bus stand or railway station.
  • 🍴 8:00 PM: Dinner (self-arranged).

Thank you all for your enthusiastic participation in this memorable trip. I hope you enjoyed the journey, creating cherished memories of nature, culture, and adventure!
